The strategies

Two systems. Two distinct behaviors. One philosophy: no emotion, no guessing, no forcing trades that are not there.

Opening-session logic

Easora

Easora is built around liquidity movement at the market open. It looks for the moments when early-session liquidity is swept and price reveals momentum opportunity.

  • Designed around the opening session.
  • Focuses on sweeps, liquidity, and momentum behavior.
  • Built to exploit rule-based opportunities, not emotional reactions.
Structure-continuation logic

Structora

Structora is built around structured continuation in the market. It looks for organized price development and acts when the structure confirms the continuation logic.

  • Built on rule-based market structure.
  • Looks for disciplined continuation rather than random entries.
  • Represents the side of trading where process matters more than prediction.
