License Structure & Profit Share

The structure is designed so traders never need to use their trading capital to pay subscriptions. The goal is for profits generated by the strategy to cover the license.


Day 0–30 – Learning period. Install the platform, configure the strategy, and observe how it behaves.

Day 31 – $500 license.

Day 61 – $1,000 license.

Day 91 – $2,000 license.

Day 121+ – 20% of monthly net profit generated by the strategy (minimum $2,000).


The percentage-based license beginning on Day 121 is calculated based on the strategy trading one NQ contract.

License holders may choose to adjust the number of contracts traded, including micro or mini contracts, for scaling purposes.

Increasing the number of contracts may increase potential profits, but it also proportionally increases potential losses.

Users remain fully responsible for determining the position size that is appropriate for their account and risk tolerance.
